## What is IoT?

IoT refers to the network of physical devices, sensors, and software that communicate and exchange data over the internet. Devices range from everyday household items like thermostats and refrigerators to advanced industrial machines.

## Key Applications of IoT

- **Smart Homes**: Automation of lighting, security systems, and appliances.
- **Healthcare**: Remote patient monitoring through connected devices.
- **Agriculture**: Smart irrigation and livestock monitoring.
- **Industrial Automation**: Improved efficiency in factories with real-time monitoring.

## Challenges in IoT

- **Security Risks**: With more connected devices, there's an increased risk of data breaches.
- **Scalability**: Managing and updating vast networks of devices can be complex.
